About Me

Hello, my name is Patrick Hannan. I am a 19 year old college student attending the University of Southern California, but I am taking a semester off to participate in the Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp. I am majoring in Business Administration, but with the current pandemic I decided to take a semester leave of absense to learn something I wouldn't normally learn through my school's business program. As a result, I am brand new to coding.

Originally, from Los Angeles, California, my family moved to Atlanta, Georgia when I was a kid so that is where I grew up. However, I am a massive LA sports fan. My favorite thing to do is to watch the Lakers or Dodgers games on television and in person.

My other hobbies include outdoor activities golfing, playing baseball, kayaking, as well as photography. I love being outdoors, and these physical activities allow me to get exercise and hang out with friends. Photography, while not a physical activity, allows me to be creative and go out and see the world and its beauty.
